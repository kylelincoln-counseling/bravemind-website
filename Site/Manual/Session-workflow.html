Session Workflow

This section details the general, step-by-step process for preparing and running any clinical session using the BRAVEMIND Ukraine system, from initial setup to conclusion.

Session Preparation

Before starting the application, ensure that all peripheral items (e.g., HMD, audio cables, etc.) are connected to the PC and that the headset is positioned reasonably level and close to the default seated orientation of the patient to minimize camera view offset.

Patient Comfort

To minimize discomfort that could break immersion, ensure the patient is situated comfortably. Take time to fit the HMD properly to their head, adjusting the strap and IPD of the lenses as needed. A general guideline is to keep the HMD level and off the nose. Ensure the HMD cable runs over the shoulder and down the patient's back and is not restricting movement.

Simulation Sickness

As the Clinician, if you begin to feel simulation sickness from prolonged viewing of the Patient Application window, focus your eyes away from the monitor for brief periods to reduce the effect.

Session Initialization

To begin a session, first launch the Mixed Reality Portal and ensure the HMD status is 'Ready'. Then, launch the Patient Application. SteamVR will initialize, and the Clinician Controller window will appear. Arrange the windows on your screen as needed. Select a scenario and wait for it to load. Once the patient is situated with their head comfortably forward, click the 'Reset Head Tracker' button in the Controller to zero-out the field-of-view.
