System Requirements

Ensure your PC meets the following specifications to run BRAVEMIND Ukraine smoothly.

Minimum Specifications

  • OS: Windows 10/11
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4590 / AMD Ryzen 5 1500X or greater
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 / AMD Radeon R9 290 or greater

Recommended Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i7 / AMD Ryzen 7 or greater
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 20-series / RTX 30-series or greater

NVIDIA Configuration Guide for Windows 11

To optimize your system's performance for VR applications, it is recommended to configure the NVIDIA Control Panel settings.

Windows 11 Display Settings

Ensuring your display settings are correct is also important for optimal performance.

PC Launch Instructions for BRAVEMIND Ukraine

Once the Meta Quest 2 headset is connected to the PC and Oculus Link mode is active, the operator should launch the BRAVEMIND Ukraine application from the PC.
