Combat Position

This scenario simulates the experience of being in a forward combat trench. It includes environmental controls for weather, time of day, and various auditory and visual triggers relevant to this context.

Air Raid

This scenario is designed to replicate the experience of an air raid in a civilian area. The clinician can control the intensity and frequency of sirens, explosions, and other related events.

Refugees

This scenario focuses on the experience of displacement and the challenges faced by refugees, such as navigating checkpoints and crowded shelters.
