PC Application Setup

First, you will need to set up the Meta Quest Link application on your PC.

  1. Create an account, or log in with a ready-made one from Facebook or Meta.

  2. In the settings, allow apps that have not been reviewed by Meta to run. This is the 'Unknown Sources' toggle.

  3. Add your Meta Quest 2 device to your Meta profile.

  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to set up Quest Link and be sure to select the wired 'Link (Cable)' method.

  5. If everything went correctly, you should see a picture of the headset with a green indicator light, showing it is successfully added.

In-Headset Setup

Once the PC application is ready, put on the headset to complete the connection.

  1. Click on the clock in the panel to open Quick Settings.

  2. Select the 'Quest Link' tile.

  3. In this window, you should see a button with the name of your computer. Select it and press the blue 'Launch' button.

  4. This video shows the full flow of opening settings, selecting Quest Link, and setting the room boundary.

  5. This video focuses on just the boundary setup process.

  6. If all is well, the headset should show the Quest Link environment, as seen in the image and video below.

  7. From the Quest Link environment, you can now launch the BRAVEMIND Ukraine application on the PC.
