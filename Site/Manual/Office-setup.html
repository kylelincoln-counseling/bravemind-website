Meta Quest 2 Setup Overview

The below illustration shows the standard clinical office setup for a BRAVEMIND Ukraine session.

Hardware & Environment Details

The following steps detail the hardware and physical space requirements shown in the diagram.

Area 1: Client & Head-Mounted Display

  • Meta Quest 2 with Meta Quest 2 Elite Strap
  • Charging Link Cable (such as the JSAUX 18W Charging 16ft) plugged into the PC and power source.
  • Link Cable suspended overhead (pully system recommended)
  • Meta Quest controllers Left (L) and Right (R)
  • Keep spare AA batteries for the Meta Quest controllers (batteries last up to 40 hours).

Area 2: Clinician & PC Laptop

  • Connect the PC to its power cable.
  • Connect the Link Cable from the headset to a USB 3.0 port on the PC.
  • Clinical Scripts should be present either digitally (on secondary monitor) or printed

Area 3: Physical Space

  • Ensure the client has a clear, safe space of at least 2 x 2 meters (6.5 x 6.5 feet).
  • An 'X' may be taped to the center of the unobstructed area
  • For safety, the client must wear closed-toed shoes during the session. Alternately, a client may choose to sit.
